April 9 2026 Football match Rwanda Varsity League

UPI VS EAUR Rwanda Varisty League

In the spirit of sportsmanship and healthy competition, the Rwanda National University Sports Federation (FRUSports) organizes the Rwanda University League, bringing together students from various universities to showcase their athletic talents.

The match between the ULK Polytechnique Insitute (UPI) and the East African University of Rwanda (EAUR) was a thrilling encounter that showcased the passion and skill of university students. The game was played in a spirit of competition, with both teams displaying great sportsmanship and determination.

The game saw a display of impressive football skills from both teams. The midfield battle was intense, with players from both UPI and EAUR fighting for control of the ball. The defense lines were solid, with defenders making crucial tackles and interceptions to prevent goals. The goalkeepers were on top of their game, making spectacular saves to keep their teams in the game.

In the end, the match ended in a 2-4 loss for UPI as well as being elimainated in the quater finals of the Rwanda Varisty League, with the first goal scored by UPI in the first half and the equalizer and the winning goals scored by EAUR in the second half. The goal scorers were not immediately available, but their names will be updated as soon as possible.

The match was attended by a large crowd of students from both universities, who cheered on their teams throughout the game. The atmosphere was electric, with chants and songs filling the air.

The match was officiated by a team of qualified referees who ensured that the game was played in accordance with the rules of football. The referees were praised for their professionalism and impartiality.
